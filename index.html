Good question — this one shows up in every exam. Before we break it down: imagine you're a plant stuck in one place all day. How would you get your own food? You can't order from Swiggy 😀
I will cook by myself
Exactly! Just like you, plants also cook their own food. This process is called photosynthesis.
Now, what 3 things would plants need to cook their food?
Sunlight.
Correct! You need energy to cook, and sunlight is free energy ☀️
1 / 3 inputs identified
Now, plants are chefs. They mix ingredients. Sunlight is the stove. What gas from the air do you think is their main ingredient? Hint: we breathe it out when we exercise.
Oxygen?
Not quite — one more try
Close, but that's what we breathe in, not out. Think about what happens when you blow air onto a mirror — it fogs up. That gas is the one plants are after.
Water vapour?
Still not it — here's a bigger hint
Good guess, but not this one. It's a gas, it makes fizzy drinks fizzy, and you breathe it out when you exercise. Starts with "carbon..."
Carbon dioxide!
Perfect! CO₂ in, O₂ out — that's why plants are called Earth's lungs.
2 / 3 inputs identified
Last one: the stove and gas aren't enough. A chef needs water too. Plants don't have taps — where do they secretly pull water from?
Through their roots.
Nailed it! 🌱 Roots are like the plant's straws.
3 / 3 inputs identified
Quick check — don't look at your textbook. Can you tell me the three things a plant needs for photosynthesis, in your own words?
Sunlight, carbon dioxide from the air, and water from the roots.
Summary

Plants use sunlight, carbon dioxide and water to produce glucose (food) and release oxygen. This process is called photosynthesis.

✎

Common exam mistake — many students write "plants make oxygen for humans." That's true, but not the main purpose. In exams, write: "The primary purpose of photosynthesis is to produce glucose for the plant. Oxygen is a by-product."

Final challenge — if I cover a plant's leaves with black paper for 3 days, will photosynthesis happen? Why?
No, because the leaves won't receive sunlight.
