<div class="h1">Quick recap</div>

<ul class="recap-list">
<li>Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants prepare their own food.</li>
<li>Chlorophyll in the leaves traps sunlight energy.</li>
<li>Carbon dioxide enters the leaf through tiny pores called stomata.</li>
<li>Water is absorbed from the soil by the roots.</li>
<li>The process produces glucose for the plant and releases oxygen.</li>
</ul>

<div class="section-lab" style="margin-top:20px;">Formula</div>
<div class="formula-box">
Carbon Dioxide + Water + Sunlight
<span class="op">↓</span>
Glucose + Oxygen
